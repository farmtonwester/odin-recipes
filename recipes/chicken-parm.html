Chicken Parm Subs

Warm crispy chicken sandwiches coated with rich marinara sauce and cheese served on a toasted bun.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 4 frozen breaded chicken breast filets
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 16oz package frozen garlic bread, thawed
  • 1 cup tomato sauce or marinara sauce
  • 4 slices provolone cheese
  • 12 fresh basil leaves, or to taste
  • Aluminum foil

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F(220 degrees C). Spray a baking pan with vegetable oil spray.
  2. Place forzen chicken filets on the prepared pan and bake in the preheated oven for 10 to 12 minutes. Turn chicken, sprinkle 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese on each filet, and bake until crispy, an additional 10 to 12 minutes.
  3. Slice garlic bread into 4 equal portions. Open each portion and place garlic-side up on a baking sheet. Warm garlic bread in the oven about 5 minutes.
  4. To assemble the sandwiches, place a chicken filet on a piece of garlic bread, spoon 1/4 cup tomato or marinara sauce on top of the chicke, and lay a slice of provolone cheese on top of the sauce. Place 3 basil leaves, or more to taste, on each sandwich, and top with the other half.
  5. Wrap each sandwich in foil and return to the oven until the cheese is melted, 5 to 8 minutes. Unwrap and serve warm.
